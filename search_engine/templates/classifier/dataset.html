Methodology

Selection Criteria

Articles were selected based on thematic relevance to the three core categories, prioritizing text with clear cross-domain overlaps (e.g., Health-Tech, Entertainment-Business).

Quality Control

Automated duplicate removal and manual label verification by a primary annotator.
